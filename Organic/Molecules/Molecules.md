# Molecules
Organic molecules are typically characterised as molecules containing C-C and C-H bonds, which were deprived from living/ biological/ organic compounds. 

>NOTE: Nitrates carbon dioxide, ammonia bicarbonates and carbonates are inorganic. Methane and ammonia are organic.
